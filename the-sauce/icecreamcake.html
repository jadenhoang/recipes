Ice Cream Cake

Description

Get ready to make the best Ice Cream Cake you've ever tried. This will send you to a state of awwww, the mmmmms are contiguous. This will only take you 20 minutes to make, once it's in your stomach the bliss begins.

Best of all, there's only 7 ingredients!!!

Ingredients

  • vanilla ice cream
  • corn flakes
  • sugar
  • cinnamon
  • salted butter
  • whipped topping (COOL WHIP)
  • vanilla extract

Description

  1. Take the ice cream out of the freezer and let it sit on counter for about 30 minutes (until softened.)
  2. Crush and measure out your corn flakes.
  3. Add sugar and cinnamon to crushed corn flakes and stir or shake it up in the ziploc bag until thoroughly coated.
  4. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium-low heat.
  5. Once melted add the corn flake mixture. Stir constantly for about 4-5 minutes until lightly golden brown. Do not walk away and do not turn up the heat. You don’t want the sugar to burn. Take off heat and allow to cool.
  6. In a large bowl, combine softened vanilla ice cream, whipped topping and vanilla extract. Use a large spoon and stir until combined well.
  7. Spread half of the cornflake mixture into the bottom of a 9×13 baking dish. Just press down with your hand until it is evenly spread out.
  8. Then pour ice cream mixture over corn flake mixture. Spread out evenly.
  9. Finally, top with remaining corn flake mixture.
  10. Cover and pop into freezer for at least 3-4 hours to let it all set up.
  11. When ready to serve, cut into squares and serve with a drizzle of honey or chocolate and caramel sauces!
