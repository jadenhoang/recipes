Banana Pudding

Description

Get ready to make the best banana pudding you've ever tried. This will send you to a state of awwww, the mmmmms are contiguous. This will only take you 20 minutes to make, once it's in your stomach the bliss begins.

Best of all, there's only 7 ingredients!!!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Pudding mix
  • Sweetend condensed milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla wafers

It's as easy as 1234

  1. Whisk the pudding mix into the milk
  2. Add the sweetened condesned milk and vanilla
  3. Fold in the frozen whipped topping
  4. Layer the mixture with the sliced bananas and vanilla wafers.
